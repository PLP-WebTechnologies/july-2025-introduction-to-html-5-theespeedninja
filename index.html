🍫 Simple & Yummy Chocolate Cookies Recipe

🍴 Yields: About 18 cookies

⏱️ Time: 10 min prep, 10–12 min bake

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup (100g) white sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup (30g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup (130g) chocolate chips or chunks (dark, milk, or both—your call)

🔥 Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, cream the butter and sugars together until fluffy (like a whipped cloud of joy).
  3. Mix in the egg and vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Combine the dry ingredients with the wet until you’ve got a rich, chocolatey dough.
  6. Fold in those glorious chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough (about 1 tbsp each) onto the tray. Give ‘em some space—they like room to spread.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes. Don’t overbake! They might look slightly soft, but they'll firm up as they cool.
  9. Let them cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.

💡Pro Tips (because you’re not basic):

  • Want a fudgier center? Chill the dough for 30 minutes before baking.
  • Toss a few extra chocolate chips on top after baking for that bakery-look flex.
  • Add a pinch of sea salt on top before baking if you're feeling fancy. Salt + chocolate = genius.
